Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Introduction

MyKK ("we," "our," or "us") is a browser dashboard and start page extension for Google Chrome. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the MyKK extension and related services.

Information We Collect

MyKK collects and stores the following types of information:

  • Custom Settings: Your dashboard layout preferences, widget configurations, bookmarks, and visual customizations are stored locally on your device using your browser's built-in storage.
  • Extension Settings: Preferences such as theme selection, layout options, and feature toggles are stored using Chrome's storage APIs to enable syncing across your devices when signed into Chrome.

Information We Do Not Collect

MyKK is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Browsing history or browsing activity
  • Analytics or usage tracking data
  • Cookies for tracking purposes
  • Any data from other tabs, windows, or extensions

How We Use Your Information

The information stored by MyKK is used solely to provide and maintain your personalized dashboard experience:

  • localStorage: Used to store your dashboard configuration and preferences directly in your browser. This data never leaves your device unless you opt into cloud sync.
  • Chrome Sync: If you are signed into Chrome, your extension settings may be synced across your devices using Chrome's built-in sync functionality. This is managed entirely by Google's Chrome infrastructure.

Data Storage

MyKK uses the following storage mechanisms:

  • Chrome Storage APIs: Your extension settings and preferences are stored using chrome.storage.local and chrome.storage.sync, which are secure, sandboxed storage areas provided by the Chrome browser.
  • Firebase (Optional Cloud Sync): If you choose to enable cloud sync for your dashboard data, your settings are securely stored using Firebase. This is entirely optional and requires explicit opt-in.

Third-Party Services

MyKK integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Firebase: Used for optional cloud sync of dashboard settings. Firebase is operated by Google and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Stripe: Used for processing payments for MyKK Pro subscriptions. Stripe handles all payment information directly and is subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy. We do not store your payment card details.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. All data stored locally remains on your device and is protected by your browser's built-in security features. Cloud-synced data is transmitted over encrypted connections and stored securely using industry-standard practices.

Children's Privacy

MyKK is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since MyKK does not collect personal information from any users, there is no risk of inadvertently collecting children's data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Your Rights

Since MyKK stores data locally on your device, you have full control over your data at all times. You can:

  • View and modify your settings directly within the extension
  • Clear all stored data by removing the extension
  • Disable cloud sync at any time through the extension settings
  • Request deletion of any cloud-synced data by contacting us

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@mykk.us.
