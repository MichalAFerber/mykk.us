Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Overview

MyKK is built with privacy as a core principle. We collect the minimum data necessary to provide our services. There are no analytics, no tracking scripts, and no ads anywhere in our products.

MyKK Dashboard (start.mykk.us)

The MyKK Dashboard is a single HTML file that runs entirely in your browser.

  • All settings, bookmarks, notes, and preferences are stored in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you opt into cloud sync.
  • No data is sent to any server during normal use.
  • The dashboard works fully offline.

Optional Google Sign-In & Cloud Sync

You may choose to sign in with your Google account to sync your dashboard settings across devices. This feature is entirely optional.

  • Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication (Google Sign-In). We receive your name, email address, and profile photo from Google.
  • When cloud sync is enabled, your dashboard settings are stored in Google Cloud Firestore, associated with your Google account.
  • You can sign out at any time, which stops all syncing. Your local data remains in localStorage.
  • We do not access your Google contacts, files, calendar, or any other Google services beyond authentication.

MyKK Chrome Extension

The MyKK Chrome Extension enhances the dashboard experience by setting it as your new tab page.

  • The extension stores your subscription status in chrome.storage.local, which stays on your device.
  • The extension uses Google OAuth for authentication when you sign in.
  • No browsing history, tabs, or other browser data is collected or accessed by the extension.

Backend API (api.mykk.us)

Our backend API handles license validation for premium features.

  • When you activate a license, we store your email address and a device identifier to validate your subscription.
  • This data is used solely for license verification and is not shared with third parties.
  • No usage data, browsing behavior, or personal content is collected by the API.

Payments

All payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store, process, or have access to your credit card numbers, bank account details, or other payment information. Stripe's privacy policy governs the handling of your payment data. You can review it at stripe.com/privacy.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools (no Google Analytics, no Mixpanel, nothing).
  • We do not serve or display advertisements.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not track your browsing history or behavior.
  • We do not use cookies for tracking. Any cookies present are strictly for authentication sessions.

Data Retention & Deletion

  • localStorage data: Controlled entirely by you. Clear your browser data to remove it.
  • Cloud sync data: You can delete your synced data by signing out and requesting deletion. Contact us to have your Firestore data removed.
  • License data: Your email and device ID are retained for the duration of your subscription. Contact us to request deletion after your subscription ends.

Third-Party Services

MyKK integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication & Firestore (Google) — for optional cloud sync
  • Stripe — for payment processing
  • OpenWeatherMap — for weather widget data (your location is sent to their API if you enable the weather widget)

Each service has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to request data deletion, contact us at support@mykk.us.
